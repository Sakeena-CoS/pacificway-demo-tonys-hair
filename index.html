Our Services

Hair and tanning, all in one place.

Tony's Hair Design & Tanning offers a full range of hair and tanning services — everything you need to look and feel your best, under one roof.

Haircuts & Styling
Precision haircuts and styling for men, women, and children — tailored to your face shape, hair texture, and personal style by our certified stylists.
Colour & Highlights
Full colour, highlights, partial highlights, and toning — applied with expertise and professional-grade colour systems for vibrant, lasting results.
Balayage
Hand-painted balayage for natural, dimensional colour that grows out beautifully. The perfect low-maintenance look that keeps you looking effortlessly styled.
UV Tanning Beds
Professional UV tanning beds for a sun-kissed glow year-round. Multiple bed options to suit your skin type and desired results — safe, clean, and effective.
Spray Tan
Professional spray tanning for an even, natural-looking bronze without UV exposure. Perfect before events, holidays, or whenever you want that sun-kissed look.
Skin & Beauty
Additional beauty treatments and skin services to complement your hair and tan. Ask us about our current offerings and seasonal beauty packages.

The Tony's Difference
